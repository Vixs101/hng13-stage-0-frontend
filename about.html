Areas Where I'm Building Confidence

Growth requires honesty about where we struggle. Here are the areas where I'm working to build more confidence:

  • Backend Development: While I'm comfortable with front-end technologies, I'm less confident with server-side programming, databases, and API design. I'm actively working to strengthen these skills.
  • System Design: Understanding how to architect large-scale applications and make decisions about infrastructure is still a learning area for me.
  • Testing: Writing comprehensive tests (unit, integration, e2e) is something I know is crucial, but I need more practice to make it second nature.
  • Algorithm Complexity: While I can solve many coding challenges, I sometimes struggle with optimizing solutions and analyzing time/space complexity confidently.
  • Speaking Up in Technical Discussions: I occasionally hesitate to share my ideas in group settings, worried they might not be "right enough." I'm working on being more vocal and trusting my perspective.
